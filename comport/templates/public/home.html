Change the conversation with transparency.

Comport is a tool for law enforcement agencies to open their data and be accountable to their citizens.

Law enforcement agencies can plug in existing internal affairs data, add contextual information about policies and programs, and quickly create an open data site. It is compatible with IA Pro, the industry-leading professional standards software.

Share information to change the conversation in your community from debating problems to discussing solutions.
